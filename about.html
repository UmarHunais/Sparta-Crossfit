ABOUT US

WE ARE THE PHALANX.
WE ARE SPARTA.

Founded on the principles of discipline, grit, and athletic excellence, SPARTA is Sri Lanka's premier destination for functional fitness and CrossFit. We don't believe in machines; we believe in building the human machine.

Our box is designed for performance. From Olympic lifting platforms to custom functional rigs, every inch of SPARTA is built to forge warriors. Whether you're a beginner or a competitive athlete, you'll find a community that pushes you to your absolute limits.

  • Train from beginner to expert level
  • Modern world-class facility
  • 24/7 support and guidance
  • Certified professional trainers
